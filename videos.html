videos.



In this section you'll find two categories of videos -- film analyses and travel montages. I'm also working on a new channel where I talk about science, music theory, and any thing else that I find interesting, so stay tuned for that!



Film Analysis


My friends will probably tell you that I start too many conversations with "wait, have you seen...?". Growing up, my father would show our family one of his favorite movies every Friday night. Once I started analyzing films as part of my high school English courses, you could say that everything just clicked together and a new passion was born.




Montage


It can be tough to get into the habit, but taking a few seconds of video every time I see something cool has allowed me to keep track of memories a lot more easily.
